WEED DETECTION

Agriculture, essential for human sustenance, has faced challenges with the increasing population demanding higher productivity. Traditional methods like using cow dung as fertilizer sufficed in the past. However, the pursuit of higher profits led to the Green Revolution, marked by increased herbicide usage, causing environmental damage. To address this, we've implemented methods to reduce herbicide usage by detecting weed areas in field images using MATLAB. As land and resources diminish, precision agriculture gains prominence, with image processing offering solutions to optimize productivity sustainably.
